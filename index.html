Transportation

Transport or transportation is the movement ot people, animals and goods from one place to another.
Transportation is important because it enables trade among people which is essential for the development of civilization.

Means Of Transport

The modes through which we travel are called the means of transportation. The means of transport can be broadly divided into three types: land, water and air.

Land Transportation

The means or mode of transportation that function on the land are categorised under the land transportation
Cars , trucks , scooters , buses , etc. are the examples of land transportation. These vehicles move on wheels and can go around anywhere on the land.
This category of transportation is used in everyday life and is also cost effective.

Land transport can be divided into railways and roadways

Railways

Railways include the modes of travelling that run on tracks i.e. trains. The trains are a cost effective and fast means of travel.
They are faster than the roadways. In India, the first train was introducd by the British in 1853. The trains travel across almost all the major cities, towns and other regions as they have well-connected network. Nowadays, various fast trains have been started. The trains have good facilities of food and beverages, comfort, medication, etc. Shatabdi Express is the fastest train in India. Indian railways is the biggest railway system of Asia and fourth largest railway system of the worlds

Roadways

Roadways include the modes of travelling that run on the roads and highways.
Roads work as arteries of the economic, social and cultural healthh of a country by transporting men and materials from one place to another.Various highwaysand expressways are built to enable smooth roadways.
Broad and straight roads which have about 4 or 5 lanes are called freeways or expressways. The highways connect the roads of the border. The state/national highways connect the state borders i.e. one state with another the while the international highways connect the country borders i.e. one country with another.
